EVO PEAK

ANCIENT TRILOBITE

HP 5,300
EXOSKELETON APEX CLASS // LENGTH: 70cm Max
An ancient, hard-shelled marine arthropod that ruled the ocean floors for nearly 300 million years before vanishing in Earth's greatest mass extinction.
Core Concepts
  • Evolutionary Icons: One of the most successful early animal groups, diversifying into over 20,000 distinct species.
  • The Three Lobes: Their body structure isn't split head-to-tail, but rather vertically into three distinct left-to-right sections.
  • Extinction Markers: They completely vanished during the Permian-Triassic "Great Dying" event.
Key Vocab
  • Cephalon: The distinct, hardened head shield area of the trilobite body structure.
  • Chitin: The tough, glucose-based material that formed their protective exoskeletons.
  • Calcite Eyes: Unique visual organs made of pure crystal lenses—the first complex eyes in nature.
Cosmic Connection
The Horseshoe Crab Lineage: Look closely at a modern Horseshoe Crab today! While not direct descendants, they share the exact same armored, bottom-dwelling blueprint and niche that trilobites pioneered half a billion years ago.
